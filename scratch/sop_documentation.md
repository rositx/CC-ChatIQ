## 3. Customer Care Operational Policies

### 3.1 Refund Policy (Hard Rules)

* **Eligibility Window:** Customers are eligible for a full refund within **30 days** of the confirmed delivery date.
* **Condition Requirements:** The item must be returned in its original packaging with all accessories (ear tips, cables, case/pouch) intact.
* **Shipping Fees:** If the return is due to customer preference (change of mind), the customer covers return shipping. If the return is due to a documented factory defect, our company provides a prepaid shipping label.
* **Processing Timeline:** Refunds take **5–7 business days** to clear back to the original payment method once our warehouse scans the returned package.

### 3.2 Replacement & Warranty Policy

* **Warranty Period:** Both models (**T001** and **P002**) carry a **1-Year Limited Manufacturer's Warranty** covering hardware malfunctions, firmware failure, battery degradation over 30%, and factory defects.
* **Exclusions:** Physical damage, water damage exceeding IP ratings, modifications, and loss of individual earbuds are strictly excluded.
* **Cross-Shipping:** We do not offer cross-shipping. The defective product must be received and evaluated at our center before a replacement unit is dispatched.

## 4. Step-by-Step Resolution Workflows

### 4.1 Refund Workflow

```
[Customer Requests Refund] 
       │
       ▼
[Verify Order Date via CRM] ──(> 30 Days)──> [Deny Refund / Offer Troubleshooting]
       │
   (< 30 Days)
       │
       ▼
[Confirm Condition & Packaging]
       │
       ▼
[Issue RMA Number & Return Shipping Instructions]
       │
       ▼
[Warehouse Inspection & Scan] ──> [Process Refund via Payment Gateway (5-7 Days)]

```

### 4.2 Replacement Workflow

1. **Identify Malfunction:** Ask targeted questions to confirm the failure type (e.g., *Left earbud T001 not charging*, or *ANC humming on P002*).
2. **Enforce Baseline Troubleshooting:** (See Section 5 for scripts).
3. **Warranty Verification:** If troubleshooting fails, check the serial number/invoice.
4. **RMA Generation:** Generate a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) tracking slip. Issue a prepaid shipping label if within the 1-year coverage window.
5. **Dispatch Unit:** Upon warehouse receipt of the damaged item, ship the new replacement unit via ground courier.

### 5.3 Technical Fault & Replacement Inquiries (Scripts)

#### Scenario D: T001 Left/Right earbud charging issue

> **Agent Script:**
> "I'm sorry to hear that your T001 earbuds are experiencing charging issues. Let's run a quick 2-step reset to fix this right away, as it's usually just a connection desynchronization issue:
> 1. Take a lint-free cotton swab dipped in a tiny amount of rubbing alcohol and gently clean the metallic charging pins inside the case and on the base of the earbuds to clear invisible sweat debris.
> 2. Place both buds into the case, leave the lid open, and press and hold the button on the back of the case for 10 seconds until the LED indicators flash amber three times.
> 
> 
> Please let me know if the charging indicator turns green after trying those steps. If it stays dark, I will instantly generate a prepaid warranty replacement label to swap your unit out."

#### Scenario E: P002 ANC Bluetooth dropouts

> **Agent Script:**
> "I apologize for the connectivity disruptions you are facing with your P002 headphones. Let's perform a hard factory reset to re-calibrate your Bluetooth pairing stack:
> 1. Go to your device's Bluetooth menu, select the P002, and click 'Forget Device' or 'Unpair'.
> 2. With the headphones powered off, press and hold both the Power Button and the ANC Toggle Button simultaneously for 7 seconds until you hear a dual-tone chime.
> 3. Turn your phone's Bluetooth toggle off, turn it back on, and attempt to pair fresh.
> 
> 
> If the audio continues to cut out after resetting the device, let me know your shipping address so we can initiate our official warranty exchange protocol for you."

## 6. Support Checklist for Agents

* Always confirm the **model identifier (T001 or P002)** before dispensing technical instructions.
* Cross-verify the **delivery date and order proof** in your workspace CRM profile before authorizing return shipping labels.
* Tag every ticket correctly under `[PRODUCT-INFO]`, `[REFUND]`, or `[WARRANTY-REPLACEMENT]` classifications to keep team performance metrics clear.
* Never guarantee an instant cash back settlement; always manage customer expectations by quoting the mandatory **5–7 business days** warehouse bank clearing cycle.
